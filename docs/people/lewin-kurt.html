Kurt Lewin

Figure 1. Kurt Lewin himself

Facts

  • 1890-1947

  • German-American

  • Psychologist

  • As a young German Jewish, fled in the 1930s from the Nazi regime to the US.

  • Taught Leon Festinger as a student.

Achievements

  • Founding father of (modern experimental) social psychology (together with Fritz Heider).

  • Founder of Gestalt Psychology.
