Paul Broca

Figure 1. Paul Broca himself

Facts

  • 1824-1880

  • France

  • Physician, anatomist, anthropologist

Achievements

  • Contributed coming up with the concept of localization of function in the brain

  • Region of the (usually left) frontal lobe is called after him, the Broca’s area, which is responsible for speech production.

    • Published evidence that injury to specific brain areas in the left hemisphere will lead to loss of ability to speak (Broca’s/expressive aphasia), but not to other mental abilities.
