Book

We use the book "Psychology" (8th edition) by Peter Gray and David F. Bjorklund (IBAN: 9781319015893).

Figure 1. Course book "Psychology"

The content (in alignment with the book) is split into 7 parts and 16 chapters in total, which are:

  1. Foundations

    1. Background

    2. Methods

  2. Behavior based on Biology

    1. Genetics, Evolution

    2. Neural Control

    3. Motivation, Emotion

  3. Sensation, Perception

    1. Senses

    2. Vision

  4. Learning, Thinking

    1. Learning

    2. Memory, Attention, Consciousness

    3. Reasoning, Intelligence

  5. Development

    1. Body/Thought/Language Development

    2. Social Development

  6. Social, Personality

    1. Social Psychology

    2. Personality

  7. Disorder, Treatment

    1. Disorders

    2. Treatments

Each chapter comes along with a set of focus questions (about 40 each). Someone from the course made a compiled list (PDF, 700kb) of all focus questions in a single document.
