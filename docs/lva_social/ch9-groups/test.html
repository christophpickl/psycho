    4. Social loafing prevents us from new people/experiences.

  2. What are social norms?

    1. Behaviors expected when in a certain position.

    2. Behaviors that promote mutual liking, and bind members together.

    3. A powerful behavioral determinant.

    4. Member listening to each other’s opinion.
