Self-Test on Attitudes

1. Nature and Origin

  1. Which conclusions are most consistent with heritability of attitudes?

    1. shaped by surroundings, no genetic component

    2. inherited and dictated by genes, little environmental factors

    3. inherit temperament/personality, likely to develop similar attitudes of genetic relatives

    4. fraternal twins same as identical twins

      • c.

  2. Our emotional reaction to an object is called a __ component of attitudes.

    1. affective

    2. behavioral

    3. cognitive

    4. operant

      • a.

  3. Which attitude is about examining facts and weighing objective value?

    1. affective

    2. behavioral

    3. cognitive

    4. operant

      • c.

  4. Hearing childhood cartoon theme song, you feel happy and excited, is?

    1. classical conditioning

    2. operant conditioning

    3. self-perception

    4. values

      • a.

  5. You like dogs over cats because 1) friendly 2) create warm feeling 3) watching lots of dog videos.

    1. 1) cognitive 2) behavioral 3) affective

    2. 1) affective 2) cognitive 3) behavioral

    3. 1) cognitive 2) affective 3) behavioral

    4. 1) behavioral 2) affective 3) cognitive

      • c.

    2. attitudes not a reliable predictor of behavior

    3. the less accessible an attitude, the more likely shape behavior

  2. Highly accessible attitudes help predict:

    1. deliberate behavior

    2. future behavior

    3. spontaneous behavior

    4. intention
